What is HAkron 3000?

HAkron 3000 is a hackathon hosted by The University of Akron that is planned by students, for students!

We encourage those interested to come ready to learn new things in a fun environment, make new friends, and create something cool enough to talk about in future interviews! We accept all makers, designers, creators, engineers, programmer. If you want to make something cool, that's cool with us. Not only do we want you to succeed, we will assist you with the help of Mentors (THERE SHOULD BE A LINK OR SOMETHING HERE FOR THE MENTORS?) and our own HAkron Officers!

Some History

We're a little new to the hackathon thing, but with this being our fourth hackathon in a year, we aren't letting that stop us!

We have had a few people that liked us! Our own university made some wonderful remarks about our very first 24 hour hackathon, you can read them here.
