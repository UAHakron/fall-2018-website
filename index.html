CALLING ALL

When: February 24-25

Where: The University of Akron's NEW Law Building

What: One of the coolest 24 hour hackathons in Northeast Ohio!

What is HAkron 3000?

HAkron 3000 is a hackathon hosted by The University of Akron. Completely student run, so you know it's going to be good.

Come ready to learn new things, make new friends, and create something so awesome that even your great aunt Dorothy will be interested in it.

If you want to make something cool, this is the place to do it! Not only do we want you to succeed, we're going to help you do it.

ok great, but who are you guys?

HAkron knows hackathons. With this being our fourth hackathon in the past year, our team has the experience to deliver the best event possible!

Don't just take our word for it; See what the Akron Beacon Journal, Cleveland.com and UA's website had to say about our last hackathon.

faq

How can I participate? What would I need?

Any currently enrolled college student, or anyone who has graduated within 12 months can participate. High School students are also eligible with the proper release forms. Here's how: First, you sign up! You can sign up by pressing the sign up botton on the top of the website. Second, you show up on February 24th, ready to create something awesome! It is advised that you bring any hardware that you would like to use in your hack. As well as a computer and all the necessary cables. We'll also have some rentable hardware courtesy of MLH Hardware Lab!

What should I bring?

Student ID, clothes, toiletries, hacker setup, an idea, and anything else you need to function independently for 24 hours. Oh, and a smile!

How much does it cost?

Nothing! Zip! Nada! Attending is absolutely FREE! Free swag, free food, what more could you want?!

Do I need to come with a team?

Nope! If you do not have a team we will help you find one. (Max of 4 people per team)
