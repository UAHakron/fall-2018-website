CALLING ALL

Why should I sponsor HAkron 3000?

HAkron 3000 is the perfect recruiting and marketing event to attract the best and brightest college students to your company. All these students are hungry for new opportunities, so come and provide some to them!

Cool, but who are you people?

We are a group of college students who are dedicated to learning about technology and teaching others as we go. Check out our club website to see what we're all about, and info on past events


Don't just take our word for it; See what the Akron Beacon Journal, Cleveland.com and UA's website had to say about our past events.

sponsor faq

What can I expect if I attend?

200+ motivated and talented college students looking to gain real-world experience by building on their technical expertise and teamwork skills. This is a great opportunity to interact with job seekers and the future of engineers, entrepreneurs, and scientists of NEO and other surrounding areas!

What should I bring as a sponsor?

1. A table cloth to fit a 6ft table

2. Swag to hand out to participants (t-shirts, water bottles, pens, stickers, etc.)

3. Promotional material for your company (products, table runner, banners, etc.)

4. Technical mentors to aid students with their projects

5. A smile!

What if I can only be present for part of the event?

Of course! The best time to be present is during the beginning. This is when the most students are present and looking around the event.

What if I can't make it at all?

We understand. Any support you give us and our cause is still greatly appreciated!
